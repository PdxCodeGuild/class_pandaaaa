Short biography:

MJK is an american rock singer, actor, songwriter, music producer, author, and owner of a winery. His bands include Tool, A Perfect Circle, and Puscifer. He doesn't exactly "like" his fans and they love him for that. His parents divorced when he was a child and he was forced to live with his father. MJK went to West Point and served in the military before he was able to attend his dream school. After all of that he became a musician.
